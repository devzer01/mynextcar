About us

My Next Car.lk is a Limited Liability Company registered under the laws of Sri Lanka and established in November 2013. The new fundamental business model for www.mynextcar.lk is to become the leading automobile sales listing service and a comprehensive network of linked websites that deliver a comprehensive insight about different types of vehicles. The ultimate objective is to build mynextcar.com into a leading classified listing service in the Sri Lankan automobile sales listing market. The best way to achieve this goal is to provide the most views per listing of any service.

Vision

To be the leading automobile trading website in Sri Lanka.

Mynextcar.lk will focus on key Elements to satisfy your needs.

  • +Find a used car – browse and search registered vehicles
  • +Find a new car – browse and search unregistered & Brand New vehicles
  • +Sell your car
  • +Auto Directory listing vehicles by manufacturer
  • +Research a car
  • +Get news and events on automotive scene in Sri Lanka
  • +Car reviews
  • + Car Garage Directory / Service Directory
  • + Spare Parts Directory

Our most important group of customers are Private sellers, however we intend to divulge more attention into dealership sales and other related automobile service information. Mynextcar.com will generate immediate leads and sales by leveraging massive marketplace and other multi-channel linked websites.
